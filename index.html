About Beach Star Hotel

The Beach Star Hotel is a well-established, family-run hotel dedicated to providing unforgettable holiday experiences. Set in a peaceful, serene area, it offers the ultimate balance: just a five-minute walk to Sidari main street where you will find plenty to do and see, but far enough out that you aren't bothered by any noise.

Our hotel is situated just across from the pristine beach and features its own private swimming pool with plenty of free sunbeds to choose from. Delicious food and snacks are served all day long, either inside the hotel premises or right across the road directly by the refreshing pool area. If you are looking for a completely relaxing break, look no further!
